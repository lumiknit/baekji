# Welcome to Baekji

Baekji is a minimal writing environment designed to get out of your way.

No distractions. No clutter. Just you and your words.

---

Your writing is organized into **Projects**, each containing **Groups** and **Sheets**. Think of a sheet as a single document, a group as a folder, and a project as everything related to one piece of work.

To get started, tap any sheet in the sidebar to open it, or create a new one using the **+** button.

This project is here to show you around. Feel free to delete it whenever you're ready.
