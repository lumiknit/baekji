# Projects, Groups, and Sheets

Baekji organizes your writing in three levels.

## Project

A project is the top-level container — a novel, a journal, a research collection. You can have multiple projects and switch between them from the sidebar.

## Group

A group is a folder inside a project. Use groups to organize chapters, sections, or any logical category. Groups can be nested inside other groups.

## Sheet

A sheet is a single document. This is where you actually write. Sheets live inside groups or directly at the root of a project.

---

## The Sidebar

The sidebar on the left shows the tree of your current project. You can:

- **Tap** a sheet to open it
- **Tap** a group to expand or collapse it
- **Long-press** (or drag on desktop) to reorder items
- **Shift+click** to select multiple items
- Use the **⋮** menu on any item for more options (rename, merge, export, delete)
