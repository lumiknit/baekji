# A Few Things to Know

## Your data lives on this device

All your writing is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to any server. This means:

- Clearing your browser data **permanently deletes** your writing.
- Uninstalling the browser or the app may also erase your data.
- Different browsers and devices have **separate** storage — they don't sync automatically.

## Back up regularly

Use the **Project Backup** button (cloud icon) to download a local backup file or sync to Dropbox. Do this often, especially before clearing browser data or switching devices.

## Restoring a backup

To restore, open the project list, tap **Project Backup**, and select **Load from Local** to open your backup file. You can also connect to Dropbox to load cloud backups, or import `.md` / `.txt` files directly into any group from the sidebar.

## Moving to another device

Export a backup on the old device, then import it on the new one. That's it.

## Full reset

If you ever need a clean slate, go to **Settings → Full Reset**. This deletes everything — all projects, documents, and settings — and cannot be undone. Export a backup first.
