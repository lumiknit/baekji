# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-24_

## Overview

Baekji is a local-first writing application. It runs entirely in your browser and does not communicate with any external server.

## Data Collection

**We collect nothing.** Baekji does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. There are no analytics, no tracking scripts, no advertising networks, and no third-party services embedded in the app.

## Data Storage

All your data — projects, documents, and settings — is stored exclusively on your local device using the browser's built-in IndexedDB storage.

**Important:** If you clear your browser data, cache, or site storage, all content stored by Baekji will be permanently deleted. We have no way to recover lost data and accept no responsibility for data loss caused by browser data deletion, device failure, or any other circumstance. We also accept no responsibility for data exposure or loss caused by browser extensions, user scripts, or any other software running in the browser environment, as these are outside our control.

We strongly recommend using the built-in backup (export) feature regularly to keep copies of your work.

## Export and Sharing

Some features (backup download, export, share) may save files to your local machine or share them via your device's native share mechanism. Once data leaves the app through these features, it is subject to the policies of whatever application or service you choose to share it with. We have no control over, and accept no responsibility for, how that data is handled externally.

## Third Parties

Baekji does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-processing services. All assets, including fonts, are bundled with the app and no external network requests are made.
